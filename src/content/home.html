Welcome!

A Des Plaines tradition for over 35 years, the Des Plaines Community Concert Band (DPCCB) has a vast repertoire that includes familiar concert band standards, classic Sousa marches, contemporary works, the latest pop favorites, musical theater medleys, movie scores, and more.

DPCCB is under the direction of Dr. Al Legutki and is sponsored by Mr. and Mrs. Don Moeller and the Des Plaines Park District ⇗.
