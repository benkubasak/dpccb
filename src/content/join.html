Join or Support Us

We often hear from our audience members and others that they would like to know how to contribute to our mission. Here are our suggestions.

Attend Our Concerts

As a band, our primary objective is to play music for a packed house. Please attend our concerts and spread the word about our band, our music, and upcoming concert dates with your family, friends, and colleagues.

Subscribe to Our Newsletter

By joining our mailing list, you will be alerted to our upcoming events, latest news, blog posts, and more.

Sign Up for Our Newsletter

Connect with Us

Connect with our social media accounts to share our content and further spread the word about us. Our accounts are linked at the bottom of this page.

Join Our Ensemble

The Des Plaines Community Concert Band is always open to new members. Please contact our director to get started. Ask any questions you may have or just come to a rehearsal and see how it goes. We have members of all experience and ability levels.

Volunteer

Not feeling ready to play? You can volunteer your time to give us a hand with operations, such as taking tickets or moving equipment.

Donate

We appreciate the generous donations of our sponsors and patrons. Thank you for your support.
