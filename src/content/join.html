Join or Support Us

We often hear from our audience members and others that they would like to know how to contribute to the Des Plaines Community Concert Band (DPCCB) mission. Here are some options.

Attend Our Concerts

As a band, our primary objective is to play music for a packed house. Please attend our concerts and spread the word about our band, our music, and upcoming concert dates with your family, friends, and colleagues.

Find Our Concerts

Subscribe to Our Newsletter

By joining our mailing list, you will be alerted to our upcoming events, latest news, and more.

Connect with Us

Connect with our social media accounts to share our content and spread the word about us.

Join Our Ensemble

DPCCB is always open to new members. Please contact us to get started. Ask any questions you have or make plans to attend a rehearsal and see how it goes. We have members of all experience and ability levels.
